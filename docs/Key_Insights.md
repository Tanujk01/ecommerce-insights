# Key Insights

## Sales Performance
- A small percentage of customers contributes a large share of total revenue.
- Revenue shows clear month-over-month growth with seasonal variations.

## Customer Behavior
- Repeat customers generate significantly higher revenue than one-time buyers.
- Highly active customers (5+ orders) contribute a disproportionate share of sales.

## Returns Analysis
- Certain product categories have higher return rates than others.
- Some regions show consistently higher return behavior.

## Basket & Purchase Patterns
- Multi-item orders generate higher average revenue compared to single-item orders.
- Specific category combinations frequently appear together in orders.

## Regional Performance
- Some regions have high order volumes but lower revenue, indicating price sensitivity.
- Regional revenue growth varies significantly over time.
