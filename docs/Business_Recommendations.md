# Business Recommendations

1. Focus retention efforts on highly active customers through loyalty programs.
2. Investigate high return-rate categories to improve product quality or descriptions.
3. Promote high-performing product combinations via bundling strategies.
4. Optimize pricing strategy in regions with high order volume but low revenue.
5. Reduce dependency on a small set of customers by expanding mid-tier customer engagement.
6. Monitor long-tail products to identify underperforming SKUs for rationalization.
